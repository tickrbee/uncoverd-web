Why did **Broadcom** drop ~13% on an earnings *beat*? Because the business beat, but the stock was priced for a *raise* that never came. Broadcom (NASDAQ: AVGO) was the market's biggest mega-cap mover on June 4, 2026 — and it dragged the whole chip sector with it.

## The move

AVGO fell **-12.86% to $417.58** (from $479.23), shedding roughly **$280 billion** of market value in a session — though it's still a **~$2.0 trillion** company. The drop alone is bigger than most companies are worth.

## What's driving it

This was a "beat‑but‑guide‑light" reaction, not a miss:

- **Q2 FY2026 beat:** revenue ~$22.2B (**+48% YoY**), non‑GAAP EPS $2.44 vs. ~$2.40 expected, with free cash flow and profit growing even faster.
- **But the AI guidance gapped:** Broadcom guided **~$16B** of Q3 AI revenue, below the **~$17.3B** the Street wanted, and **reiterated** (didn't raise) its **FY2027 $100B+** AI target — a number analysts said the market had already priced higher.
- **Margins stepped down** from 77.1% to 74%, and management declined to lift the AI outlook "in a market clamoring for beats and raises."
- The cautious read spilled into **Micron, Marvell and AMD** — a sector‑wide chip selloff.

## The fundamentals

uncoverd rated AVGO **C+** going in: **Value 2/5** (expensive), **Growth 3/5**, **Profitability 3/5**, **Health 3/5**, **Momentum 2/5**. That Value 2/5 is the tell — the stock was *already* priced for perfection, so an in‑line (not raised) AI forecast was always going to sting. Broadcom also pays a dividend, so a lower price lifts the yield — worth tracking via the [ex‑dividend calendar](/calendar/ex-dividend) if you follow it for income.

## Verdict: Fair

The selloff is **a valuation reset, not a broken thesis.** The business grew revenue 48% and beat — but with a 2/5 Value score and an AI narrative priced for a beat‑and‑raise, the premium had to come out when guidance merely held. For quality‑focused investors, a cheaper AVGO is *more* interesting than an expensive one — if it keeps falling toward a sane multiple, it moves onto the watchlist rather than off it.
