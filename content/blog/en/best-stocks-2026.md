## What a ranking can and cannot do

**It can** eliminate the worst mistakes: companies with deteriorating balance sheets, fading momentum and rich valuations rarely rank high. **It cannot** know your horizon, your taxes, your existing portfolio, or that you panic at −20%. And it **will change** — this table is a photograph of a river. The [live ranking](/best-stocks) supersedes this article from the moment it was published, and the [dividend-focused cut](/blog/top-10-dividend-stocks-worldwide) re-runs the same arithmetic for income investors.
